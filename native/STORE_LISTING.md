## Full Description (both stores)
FRAME Medicine puts your health management in the palm of your hand. As a FRAME Medicine patient, you can:

- View your medication supply status and upcoming refill dates
- Confirm or manage your medication refills with a single tap
- Complete monthly wellness check-ins to track your progress
- Log weekly weigh-ins with trend charts to visualize your journey
- Message your care team directly through the app
- Receive timely reminders for refills, check-ins, and weigh-ins
- Sign in securely with Face ID or fingerprint after initial verification

FRAME Medicine specializes in men's health, offering personalized testosterone replacement therapy, GLP-1 weight management, and other wellness programs. This app is exclusively for current FRAME Medicine patients.

For more information, visit framemedicine.com
